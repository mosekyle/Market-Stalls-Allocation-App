The Wangige Market Stalls Allocation has been proudly serving the community since 2013. Our commitment to providing quality stalls and a secure market environment has made us a trusted choice for local businesses.

About Us

Management: Wangige Market Stalls Allocation is managed by the city council. Wangmart Sacco: The market has a sacco named Wangmart with over 200 members. Wangmart provides flexible loans for its members. Cleanliness: Rated one of the cleanest markets in the county, workers clean the market every Monday and Thursday. Location: Wangige Market is an indoor market, offering protection from the elements. Its central location in the town ensures a high market base.
